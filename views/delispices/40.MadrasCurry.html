Cooking instructions:

Cooking Instructions for chicken curry

  • Heat oil in a pan and fry onions still golden brown.
  • Add ginger paste and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  • Add Déli Spices Madras Curry Powder and tomatoes and cook till oil separate.
  • Mix Chicken pieces and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Add water and cook for 10 to 20 minutes on medium heat or till chicken is tender.
  • Add coriander, green chillies, coconut milk and simmer for 5 minutes on low flame.
  • Add Déli Spices Garam Masala and cook for another 2 minutes

Cooking Instructions for Chicken Fry:

  • Clean and cut 200 Gms fillet of fish into slices. Keep aside.
  •   Add Madras curry powder 5Gms, Besan 2Gms, Corn flour 4 Gms, Salt 1 Gms, Coriander Fresh chopped 3 Gms, Ajwain 0.5 Gms. and Lemon juice 5 GmsMarinate the fish fillet in mixture at least half an hour.
  •   Fry in hot vegetable oil. Remove and drain excess oil
  •   Sprinkle with Chaat Masala and coriander leaves chopped.

Cooking Instructions for Fish Fry:

  •   Clean and cut 200 Gms Chicken Dices. Keep aside.
  •    Add Madras curry powder 5Gms, Besan 2Gms, Corn flour 4 Gms, Salt 1 Gms, Coriander Fresh chopped 3 Gms, Ajwain 0.5 Gms. and Lemon juice 5 GmsMarinate the fish fillet in mixture at least half an hour.
  •    Fry in hot vegetable oil. Remove and drain excess oil
  •    Sprinkle with Chaat Masala and coriander leaves chopped
