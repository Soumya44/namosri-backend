Tikka Masala Gravy

Description:

  • Déli Spices Tikka Masala Gravy is made with aromatic, exotic spices and cooked in a creamy, lightly spiced, tomato sauce that is just divine

Cooking instructions:


  • In a pan add 25 Gm oil & sauté 50 Gm chopped ginger and green chilies.
  • Add 1 Kg Chicken Tikka Masala Gravy and stir for two minutes.
  • Add 1 .5 Kg of barbecued chicken tikka pieces. Stir for two to three minutes.
  • Add 1 lit.water and boil it till required thickness.
  • Add 350 Gm cream and cook for a minute.
  • Garnish with cream and chopped coriander.
